Budget Calculator

Add all the income and expenses that apply to you. Provide round and up-to-date numbers. Once finished, click the results button and we’ll give you a breakdown of your budget.

What do I need to fill out the Budget Calculator?

Exact figures are best. However, if you just want to get a general idea of your spending, you can enter your best estimates. Remember: it’s better to estimate higher rather than lower.

How does the Budget Calculator work?

Fill out all the categories that apply to you and we’ll calculate your spending against your income. Once finished, you’ll see a breakdown of your outgoings by category. Our budget planner helps you figure out exactly where your money is going – household expenses, bills, travel, etc.
